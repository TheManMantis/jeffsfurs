Our Services

Full service means storage, cleaning, repair and custom design.

Storage

Our fully insured and secured vault is state of the art, designed to insure ideal humidity and temperature. Conditions proven to increase the life of your fine furs.

Cleaning

We utilize the latest cleaning and glazing techniques that not only revive the luster to your fur, but also deep conditions guaranteeing a beautiful finish.

Repair

If your fur has any sort of damage, such as rips, loose seams, etc. have it repaired as soon as possible by a professional furrier.
Let us inspect it, asses the damage and repair it.

Remodel

A statement-making coat is a key piece for your closet. We can help you remodel and turn your fur into a modern and stylish garment. With an update your fur can look like a new one!
