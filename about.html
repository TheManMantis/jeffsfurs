About T. Jeff Haughton

A Shreveport native Jeff Haughton has deep roots in the community, his family owned and operated Jungerkes Furs, The Premier Fur Salon in Shreveport for decades. It was then when Jeff became enchanted by the craft and began learning the trade and an early age. Jeff studied and worked with some of the finest designers and craftsmen in the fur industry. His experience spans more than 40 years and includes stints with Neiman Marcus, Koslow's, and Sakowitz among others. He is a Master Furrier in every sense of the word! As a Master Furrier, Jeff can take a bundle of skins, make a pattern and make a new fur. He will also take a fur that is out of style and make it into a modern style.

Storage

Proper storage for a fur is is important. A temperature and humidity controlled environment can increase the longevity of your fur. The high humidity in our area can ruin the leather. The leather is where the true life of a fur lies, if the leather is ruined, so is the fur. We provide professional storage, fully insured and secured, that will guarantee the preservation of your fur here locally in Shreveport. Our vault is state of the art, designed to insure ideal humidity and temperature. Conditions proven to increase the life of your fine furs.

Cleaning

Professional cleaning is essential to the preservation of your fur. Cleaning prior to storing will keep your garment looking fresh and supple. We provide expert cleaning services that will restore your garment to its original splendor. We utilize the latest cleaning and glazing techniques that not only revive the luster to your fur, but also deep conditions guaranteeing a beautiful finish.
